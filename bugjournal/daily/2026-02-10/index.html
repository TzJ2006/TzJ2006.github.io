Daily Report — 2026-02-10#

Today’s Overview#

  • Completed Work: PCA+UNI+STAIG fusion clustering was performed on 11 spatial transcriptomics slides. The processed slides were handled using a caching mechanism.
  • Implementation: A batch processing script was initiated through the backend Bash task. ARI, NMI, and Silhouette metrics were calculated for each slide, and visualization results with metric annotations were generated using scanpy.
  • Significance: Successfully obtained clustering performance data for 7 slides (with highest ARI of 0.48). Data issues were identified in 4 slides (missing spatial coordinates, image errors, model crashes), providing insights for future method improvements.

A PCA+UNI+STAIG fusion spatial transcriptomics benchmark test was conducted on the DCC cluster. Successfully processed 7 out of 11 slides, and visualization results with metric annotations were generated for all slides.

Architecture and Strategy#

  • ✅ PCA+UNI+STAIG Fusion Batch Benchmarking — Spatial transcriptomics clustering tests were performed on 11 slides (151508-151676) of the DLPFC dataset. ARI/NMI/Silhouette metrics were calculated, and visualization results were saved. Slides 151508/151509 in the cache were skipped; the remaining 9 slides were reprocessed.

Implementation and Fixes#

  • ✅ Generating Visualization Results with Metrics — PNG visualization results were created for 7 processed slides, showing ARI/NMI/Silhouette metrics. The results were saved in the outputs/benchmark_results/pca_uni_staig_fusion/visualizations/ directory.

Issues and Solutions#

Key Issues#

1. Slides 151510 and 151672 had missing spatial coordinates, causing ValueError errors during STAIG fusion execution.#

Solution: These slides were skipped, and marked as “missing spatial coordinates” in the result table.

Key Insight: STAIG fusion requires valid spatial coordinates to function properly. Data preprocessing must ensure that all slides have accurate spatial information.

2. Slide 151676 experienced NaN losses during model training, leading to model failure.#

Solution: This slide was skipped, and recorded as a model failure.

Key Insight: Unstable values may be due to the special data distribution of this slide. It is necessary to investigate whether gradient clipping or learning rate adjustments are needed.

General Issues#

3. Slide 151674 failed due to OpenCV image reading errors.#

Solution: This slide was skipped, and the error reason was recorded.

Key Insight: Image fusion methods that rely on images require pre-verification of image file integrity.

Key Conclusions#
