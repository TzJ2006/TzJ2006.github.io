Project Progress#

MIHD Multi-modal Spatial Transcriptomics Paper (5 active days) — 🔄 Active#

Completed:

  • Completed extraction of TEDDY-G 400M full-embedding data for 11 slices and multi-strategy evaluations. It was confirmed that the STAIG framework is highly robust for gene encoders (Teddy ARI=0.495 vs PCA ARI=0.497).
  • Integrated Leiden clustering (binary search matching n_clusters) and STHD pathology GT, completed full-Leiden comparison on DLPFC (9/9 groups). The key conclusion was that high-quality embeddings are superior to clustering methods (STAIG+mclust=0.546 vs STAIG+Leiden=0.513).
  • Completed evaluation of 13 HD fusion strategies. Key negative findings included: simple fusion (concat ARI=0.191) being inferior to single-modal PCA (ARI=0.351), and adaln crashing on large-scale HD data (ARI=0.003).
  • Established the core narrative framework for the paper (Setting=No system study on zero-shot FM embedding fusion/Finding=Fusion Strategies > Simple Fusion is a Trap, Spatial Graph Learning is Key/Application=Cross-slice Retrieval). A complete bilingual paper framework was created by referring to Yuan et al. 2024 Nature Methods.
  • Created 4 GPU Slurm job scripts and 3 paper analysis scripts (cross-slice query aggregation, figure visualization, HD Marker Gene visualization). The docs/ folder was reorganized, and 12 expired scripts were archived.

Blocked Issues:

  • ⚠️ Basic contrastive HD fusion experiment is still running (48h limit). The HD learning-based fusion result set is not complete.
  • ⚠️ The root cause of the 151676 slice STAIG model collapse has not been thoroughly analyzed. It is currently handled by skipping it.
